Message from Vice Chancellor

Professor Dr. Md. Abdur Rahman Sarkar, Vice-Chancellor (VC)

I am much enthused and happy to welcome you to this web page and tend to write some sentences regarding the objectives and services of learning and education at Sheikh Fazilatunnesa Mujib University, Jamalpur which launched its function in 2013 under the provision of education ACT of private university in Bangladesh. The university provides and offers all modern subjects of study for students who aim to build their career in life. Quality education is a core motto of the authority who focuses on supporting and facilitating all necessary requirements for students. Experienced faculty members, technological facilities, learning tools, infrastructures, and modern wide library are offered for learners. Besides, extra curriculum activities and professional approach are arranged for students who have interest in shaping their life prospects are open. We are staffs and academicians are highly aware of well beings of students and committed to serving their needs. So we sincerely invite you as either parent or students to visit our campus and decide on building a career path at this university.
